Who We Are, What We Do

Leaders In The Field

Altrek Integrated Solutions Limited being essentially a farm, was birthed out of the passion for raising animals, farming and ensuring food security in the society. The dream dates back to over two decades ago. The promoters had always had passion for domestic animals. They thus kept a few at home for subsistence purpose. The number kept growing until commercial demands began to overtake the supply.

The promoters then took the initiative of having the farm registered as a limited liability company with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) in June 2016.
